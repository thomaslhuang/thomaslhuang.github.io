Tommy Huang is a designer working in type design, printed matter, digital interface, and site specific installation. Based in New York, he previously worked at Green Dragon Office in Los Angeles and Miko McGinty Inc. in Brooklyn.

He will be graduating from Yale University in Spring 2019, receiving a BA in Computing and the Arts and Graphic Design.
