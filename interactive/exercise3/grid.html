I’m at my lovers’ house. They had gotten a new puppy a couple months ago. By the time I’ve met the puppy, he’s already fully grown.

I am not me. I am bowling. There is something a bit off about the lane, and the game isn’t going well. I see a man in another lane packing up to leave, so I go over. Without speaking to him, I grab the nearest bowling ball and effortlessly toss it, netting a strike.

I was challenged to transliterate the longest word in the English language into Spanish. Despite seeing several misspellings with my own eyes, my friends told me it was correct.

Me and dad got into another argument. I am distraught, he never understands me.

I am at home. My brother claims that one of his high school friends is a stalker. He’s devised a plan to capture her at our own house. Somehow, my parents have agreed to this. She is supposed to arrive around 12:30am. I attempt to sleep through it, but wake up at 9:30pm. My brother asks me how to get into the attic. I don’t know.

I drop my phone. the screen does not crack; instead, the case begins to melt off the phone.

I’m at home. at the dinner table, with my parents and my cousin mu. she looks different; grotesque and horrifying. as if she had gotten a monstrous plastic surgery job and a terrible makeover. like a gargoyle or something.
